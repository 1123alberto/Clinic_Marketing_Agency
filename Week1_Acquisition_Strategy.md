# 7-Day Strategy & Client Acquisition System

This document summarizes our 7-day tactical rollout and outlines the long-term architecture for your automated **Client Acquisition System**.

## 🏗️ The Client Acquisition System (The "Machine")

To consistently attract and convert new patients, we are building a multi-channel funnel that leverages your distinct brand voices. The system consists of three core phases:

1. **Traffic Generation (The Top of the Funnel)**
   - **Dentplant (High-Intent)**: Dominating local searches via Google Business Profile (AEO) and eventually Google Search Ads for high-value terms (e.g., "Implant clinic Athens").
   - **Cosmetic (High-Volume/Discovery)**: Utilizing Instagram Reels and visual aesthetics to capture local attention and build desire for cosmetic procedures.

2. **The Conversion Mechanism (The Middle of the Funnel)**
   - Driving all traffic to highly optimized, frictionless landing pages on the primary `dentplant.gr` website. This is critical. By reducing the steps to book an appointment, we significantly increase the conversion rate of the traffic generated in Phase 1.

3. **Trust & Authority (The Core)**
   - Maintaining the strict "Voice Firewall" ensures that a surgical patient feels safe and a cosmetic patient feels excited and pampered. Compliance with EOO guidelines ensures long-term regulatory safety.

## 📅 The 7-Day Strategic Roadmap

This 7-day sprint is designed to lay the organic foundation before any paid advertising scales the system.

### ✅ Day 1: Foundation & Cleanup (Completed)
- **Dentplant**: Removed non-compliant brand bleed; established "Clinical Authority" with an authentic surgical suite GBP update.
- **Cosmetic**: Launched the "Aesthetic Luxury" voice via a 24-hour Instagram Story.

### ⏳ Day 2: Authority & Engagement (In Progress)
- **Dentplant**: High-value "All-on-4 / Full Arch" GBP update targeting complex restorative patients, linking directly to your new `booking.html`.
- **Cosmetic**: A 15-second "Aesthetic Consultation" Instagram Reel Script to drive algorithm discovery.
- *(Waiting on user photos/clips to execute).*

### 🗓️ Day 3: Social Proof & Trust Signals
- **Dentplant**: A GDPR-compliant "Patient Experience" post on Google Business Profile, focusing on a pain-free, technology-driven surgical recovery.
- **Cosmetic**: An Instagram Carousel showcasing "The Smile Journey" (Aesthetic transformation), heavily relying on high-end visuals.

### 🗓️ Day 4: The Funnel Audit (Infrastructure)
- **Action**: A technical day where the Manager reviews the main website (`dentplant.gr`) integrations across all social profiles. We will verify that Instagram Bio links and GBP buttons are capturing traffic effectively and routing to the correct conversion points.

### 🗓️ Day 5: Education & FAQ
- **Dentplant**: "What to expect during your implant surgery" – a clinical, reassuring GBP update addressing common patient fears.
- **Cosmetic**: "Veneers vs. Whitening" – an interactive Instagram Story poll to engage followers and generate direct message (DM) inquiries.

### 🗓️ Day 6: Local Immersion
- **Dentplant**: Highlight a clinical credential, continuing education, or a specific advanced technology used in your Athens clinic.
- **Cosmetic**: "Behind the Glow" – a lifestyle Instagram Reel showing the premium amenities of your waiting room and staff interactions.

### 🗓️ Day 7: Performance Review & Scaling Strategy
- **Action**: Analyze engagement metrics from the first 6 days. Based on the data, we will draft the blueprint for **Phase 2: Paid Acquisition** (Google Ads for Surgical, Meta Ads for Cosmetic).
