# 📸 Protocol: Authentic Visual Integration

**Objective:** To prioritize real-world clinic photography over synthetic AI generation to maintain "Local Trust," "Clinical Transparency," and "Medical Authenticity" for the Greek market.

## 📂 Asset Repository Hierarchy:
The Graphic Designer must always scan `/03_Visual_Assets/Clinic_Photos/` before initiating a new image generation. 

1.  **Level 1: Raw Authentic (Priority)**
    - Use actual photos for Google Business Profile and Instagram Stories to show the "Real Clinic."
    - Action: Basic color correction and watermark placement only.
2.  **Level 2: AI-Enhanced Reference**
    - Use a real clinic photo as a "Seed" for the image engine.
    - Action: "Enhance lighting to professional studio quality while maintaining the exact architectural layout of our actual treatment rooms."
3.  **Level 3: Procedural Hybrids**
    - Use real photos of clinic equipment (e.g., the 3D Scanner) but use AI to generate clean, abstract overlays or diagrams explaining the technology.
4.  **Level 4: Pure AI (Emergency Only)**
    - Use only if no real asset exists. Visuals must be styled to match the "Boutique Athens" vibe of the existing folder assets.

## ⚖️ Compliance & Ethics (EOO/GDPR):
- **Anonymization:** Automatically detect and blur any visible patient names on screens, paper files, or medical waste containers.
- **No Surgical Gore:** All surgical imagery (Dentplant) must be clean, blood-free, and focus on the technology or the final "healed" result.
- **Authenticity Check:** The Manager must reject any AI-enhanced image that makes the clinic look "unrecognizable" to a local patient.

## 🎨 Brand-Specific Filters:
- **i-Smile:** Increase exposure (+10%), warm highlights, soft shadows (Luxury/Boutique feel).
- **Dentplant:** High contrast, cool tones, sharp focus (Clinical/Precision feel).
